Luis Aviles

The Power of Web APIs: Develop your Voice-Driven App

Google Home and Amazon’s Echo. What do they have in common?

These devices can be used to provide the answer to common questions, getting the local time, weather or tell them to do things.

A voice-driven web application does not just mean listening or getting a vocal response, it can be used as an assistive technology also. Modern Web APIs are changing the way to develop apps and, with the help of them, we can move forward to develop apps that not only recognize what your users are saying but speak back to them as well.

We’ll see a demo app using Web APIs, design patterns through TypeScript and Angular to learn some basics of speech recognition and speech synthesis.
